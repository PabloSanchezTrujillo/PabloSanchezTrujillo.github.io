A little about me. 

My name is Pablo Manuel Sánchez Trujillo. I'm a 26-years-old graduate in video games development with a master in Artificial Intelligence for video games.

Since I got my first console when I was 6, I have always been interested in playing all the games I could get my hands on. No matter the genre, I always enjoyed them in some way. As I grew older, they weren't only a way of having fun as I started having the curiosity of how they were created and if I could make my own video game someday.

This motivation has driven me to learn everything I can about creating video games and immersive experiences for other players like me. Learning and doing as many projects as I could to improve my skills and create the best experience I can offer.

This passion and self-motivation for video games have brought me to where I am today and helped build the person I am.
