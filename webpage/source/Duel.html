Duel - WIP

Genre:

 Arcade game


Engine:

 Unreal


Summary:

 Arcade game created to improve and explore my Unreal skills

Github repository



Duel is a fast-paced arcade game that puts your reflexes and aim to the test. You step into the boots of a small-town sheriff tasked with keeping the peace.

Choose your challenge:

  • Duels: Face off against five increasingly tough opponents in one-on-one showdowns.
  • Saloon Takeover: Storm the enemy-occupied saloon and eliminate all threats within the time limit. One misstep could be fatal.
  • Local Multiplayer: Challenge friends to a duel with customizable rules.
    • We've prioritized authentic gunplay, using simple yet satisfying controls. Cock your revolver with the right stick and fire with the right trigger for a realistic feel.

This is a work-in-progress project designed to hone my Unreal Engine skills and experiment with its capabilities.


My contribution to this project

I was responsible for all coding aspects of the game, including scene setup.

I combined C++ and Blueprints to leverage the strengths of both. Complex gameplay mechanics were primarily implemented in C++ for performance and flexibility, while visual elements and scene logic were handled in Blueprints for rapid prototyping and iteration.

This approach allowed for efficient development due to the well-suited nature of each language to its respective tasks.
